Hot Meals, Relief Operations

November 14, 2024

By Admin

Food and Kitchen Equipment Drive

In light of the recent disaster that has affected parts of Camarines Sur, we are reaching out to seek your kind assistance in providing essential support to the affected communities. Many families have been displaced and are in urgent need of food supplies and kitchen equipment to help them regain a sense of normalcy in their daily lives. Your contributions, whether in the form of non-perishable food items, cooking utensils, or other kitchen essentials, will make a significant difference in ensuring that these families can prepare meals and sustain themselves during this challenging time.
