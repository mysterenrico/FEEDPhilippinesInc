Our Story

FEED Philippines Inc., Non-Profit Organization

After Supertyphoon Yolanda devastated Region VIII and surrounding areas in 2013, volunteers from across the Philippines rallied together to offer a beacon of hope—serving hot, nutritious meals to those in need. What began as a simple act of kindness in the aftermath of a tragedy has grown into a movement of resilience and compassion.

Our Mission

Delivering hot and nutritious meals to displaced individuals and communities affected by natural calamities and conflict

Hot Meals, Relief Operations

November 14, 2024

By Admin

FEED Phils Inc. on a mission to provide hot meals to parts of CamSur

Bicol remains devastated by floods after Typhoon Kristine, with many stranded without food or water for two days. FEED Philippines Inc. is setting up a kitchen in Rinconada to provide urgent meals, but we need your help to make it happen. Your generous cash donations will allow us to act swiftly and bring relief to those in desperate need. Please consider making a donation now to support this critical mission

Food, Water, Shelter

November 14, 2024

By Admin

Tubig!

In most of the barangays we've been to, the ones that received little or no help, the ones that remained flooded the longest (some still are), there is still no available potable drinking water. It is what the residents are requesting for, even more than food. Your donation of ₱200 (₱168 for the 5-gallon container, and ₱25 for the water) will provide one family with clean drinking water for several days. Help us help them.
